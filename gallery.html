VIRATY KOHLI

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. Kohli is known for his exceptional technical skills, athleticism, and passion for the game. He has numerous records to his name, including the fastest century by an Indian cricketer in ODIs and the most centuries in international cricket by an Indian batsman. Kohli is also a prolific run-scorer in all formats of the game, with over 20,000 runs in international cricket. He is a role model for millions of young cricketers around the world and is widely respected for his dedication, work ethic, and leadership qualities.

MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is a former Indian international cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest finishers in the history of cricket. Dhoni is known for his calm and composed demeanor on the field, which earned him the nickname "Captain Cool." He led the Indian team to numerous victories, including the 2007 ICC World Twenty20 and the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup. Dhoni is also a skilled wicket-keeper and is known for his lightning-fast stumping abilities. He is a legendary figure in Indian cricket and is widely respected for his leadership, skill, and sportsmanship.

ROHIT SHARMA

Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer and one of the most successful opening batsmen in the world. Known for his elegant strokeplay and ability to score big runs, Sharma has established himself as a key player for the Indian national team. He is the vice-captain of the Indian ODI team and has led the team to several victories in the absence of regular captain Virat Kohli. Sharma is also the captain of the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and has led the team to multiple championship titles. With over 14,000 runs in international cricket, Sharma is one of the most prolific run-scorers in the world and is widely regarded as one of the best opening batsmen in the game.
